Wheat Flour (Maida)

Maida is basically the Indian term which is used for differentiating the coarse fine powder obtained from wheat flour. It is also known as all-purpose flour. Maida is the endosperm part and it is rich in carbohydrates.

Used to prepare an assortment of dishes like Naan, Parathas, Cakes and biscuits.

Fine white Haida Flour is prepared by milling of wheat grains. Before milling the bran is separated and then passed through a sieve to obtain the fine white powder known as Haida. Khyant Global is one of the premier suppliers and exporters of top-quality plain Maida flour to countries across the Globe.
